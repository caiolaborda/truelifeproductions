Synopsis

An upcoming, glamorous staging of Oscar Wilde’s classic comedy. Set against a backdrop of Victorian high society, our production brings Wilde’s sharp satirical wit to life with dynamic modern performances. True Life Productions explores themes of identity, societal expectation, and double-lives in this fresh and fast-paced staging.
