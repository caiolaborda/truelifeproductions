Workshops & Training

TLP is dedicated to nurturing local talent. Explore our community drama and movement workshops for children, designed to build confidence, sharpen stage skills, and foster collaboration.

Theatre Club (Ages 7–12)

The theatre class for kids (7 to 12 years old) aims to introduce young children to the basics of theatre and dramatic arts through fun, engaging, and interactive activities. The class focuses on fostering creativity, teamwork, and self-expression while teaching essential theatre skills such as voice projection, movement, character development, and storytelling.

Led by TLP's Artistic Directors, it focuses on developing an understanding of basic theatre concepts, enhancing communication and social skills, encouraging creativity, and building confidence and teamwork abilities.

Join Our Creative Registry

Even if there are no active roles fitting your profile right now, we encourage all creatives to join the TLP Registry. This is our primary directory when sourcing cast members, lighting designers, sound engineers, set builders, stage crew, and production assistants for upcoming plays, TLP Studio showcases, and workshops.

Members of our registry receive exclusive priority alerts for casting calls, audition schedules, local theatre workshops, and professional placement opportunities before they are published publicly.

  • 🎭 Actors: Store your headshot and Spotlight link directly in our files.
  • ⚙️ Technicians: Be notified of freelance set building, lighting design, and stage management opportunities.
  • ✍️ Writers: Register your interest in regional new-writing play festivals.
