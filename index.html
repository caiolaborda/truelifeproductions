WELCOME TO TLP

True Life Productions

Welcome to True Life Productions CIC (TLP), a professional theatre company working with emerging local talent to create truth-based performances and maximise human connection. TLP exists as a platform for promising artists, enabling them to develop their craft, gain professional experience, and grow within the creative industry.

Studio at TLP

Local & Fundraiser Theatre

Alongside our professional productions, TLP Studio is our community-focused branch dedicated to nurturing local talent across Cambridge and surrounding areas. Through community-led productions, festivals, and amateur fundraisers, TLP Studio provides opportunities for local emerging artists to develop, collaborate, and share their passion for performance.

Connecting Communities through Live performance

Based on the border of Hertfordshire and Cambridgeshire, True Life Productions (TLP) was born from a desire to create a dynamic bridge between the thriving local artistic community and the professional theatre world, particularly in London.

Our core purpose extends beyond producing theatre; we are deeply committed to the professional development of local artists, fostering inclusive performances, and celebrating local identity through workshops and community drama projects.
